Frequently Asked Questions

Check the size guide on each product page and compare the measurements with a garment you already love. For a relaxed fit, choose the larger size when you are between sizes.

Our collections use breathable cottons, soft viscose blends, linen blends, crepe, and seasonally selected knits. Fabric details are listed on every product page.

Core pieces are restocked regularly. Limited seasonal drops may not return, so save your size or join the waitlist when it appears.

Follow the care label inside each garment. As a general rule, wash inside out on a gentle cycle, avoid harsh bleach, and dry in shade to preserve colour and fabric feel.

We photograph colours as accurately as possible, but tones can vary slightly by screen. Product descriptions call out undertones and fabric finish where it helps.

Standard shipping: 3–5 business days. Express: 1–2 business days. International orders: 7–14 business days depending on destination. All orders are dispatched from our Sydney warehouse within 1 business day of being placed.

Yes — free standard shipping on all Australian orders over $50. International free shipping thresholds vary by region and are shown at checkout.

We ship to 40+ countries. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times are calculated at checkout based on your location. Please note customs duties and import taxes are the customer responsibility.

You can request an exchange or return within 7 days of delivery as long as the item is unused, unwashed, and has its original tags attached.

Email hello@mustardfashion.com with your order number and reason for return. Our team will respond within 24 hours with the next steps.

Refunds are processed within 2 business days of receiving the returned item. Funds typically appear in your account within 5–7 business days depending on your bank.

Visit the My Account page and click “Create Account”. You will need your email address and a password. You can also create an account during checkout.

On the login page, click “Forgot password?” and enter your email address. You will receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if it does not appear.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, our warehouse begins picking and packing and changes can no longer be made. Contact us immediately at hello@mustardfashion.com if you need to make a change.
