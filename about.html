The Beginning

A fashion line rooted in real life

MUSTARD was born from a simple idea: everyday clothes should feel polished without asking you to compromise on comfort. Each collection is built around wearable silhouettes, reliable fabrics, and details that earn their place.

Our Process

From sketch to wardrobe

We work with trusted mills, pattern makers, and finishing partners to refine every piece before it reaches your wardrobe.

Every garment is fit-tested for movement, drape, wash feel, and repeat wear, so it looks good beyond the first try-on.

What We Stand For

Three principles. Every decision.

Material Honesty

We choose breathable weaves, soft linings, and finishes that feel good against the body and stand up to everyday wear.

Fabric Philosophy

Thoughtful by design.

Signature materials, selected for comfort, drape, durability, and everyday ease.

I

Cotton Voile

Light, breathable, and soft against the skin, chosen for everyday dresses and easy summer layers.

II

Linen Blend

Textured without feeling stiff, giving co-ords and separates a relaxed yet polished structure.

III

Viscose Crepe

Fluid, drapey, and crease-friendly, selected for pieces that need to move from desk to dinner.
